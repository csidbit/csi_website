Little About LeaderBoard


The Leadership board is a key feature of CSI DBIT solely dedicated to monitor each members' involvement and contributions in CSI events. The most engaged members will be given additional benefits and rewards as a sign of appreciation.
The main motive behind this initiative is to encourage the members who are constantly trying to learn and acknowledge the activities conducted by CSI and work on the path to implement it.

The constraints in the point system followed to maintain fairness and transparency in the leaderboard are:
Participation
Winning
Task Completion

This applies for all events workshop and competition conducted in the student chapter
