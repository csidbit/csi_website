Description

Joomla! is a collective workshop and competition held by CSI on 30th and 31st of August, 2019. With Sarah Solkar as the speaker, this event focused on teaching participants the very popular, free and open source content management system along with its various web content applications. Winners of the competition i.e the best designers were awarded with internship offers as the prizes.
