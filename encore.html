Encore

Department of Information Technology of Don Bosco Institute of Technology (DBIT), Kurla, Mumbai successfully organized the "First ever Online National Level Paper Presentation Contest" in Mumbai University for B.E. students of Electronics & Telecommunications, Computer and Information Technology (I.T.) engineering branches.

Instructions :

  • Paper based on project has to be strictly in IEEE format.
  • Registration is free.
  • Selected Paper list will be displayed on our website.
